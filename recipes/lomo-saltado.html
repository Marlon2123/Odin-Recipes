Lomo Saltado: A Peruvian Classic

Lomo saltado is a classic dish of Peruvian cuisine. It is a stir-fry of beef with vegetables, french fries, and white rice. The beef is cut into thin strips and marinated in garlic, salt, and pepper. It is then stir-fried in a wok or large skillet with hot oil until browned on all sides. Vegetables, such as onion, tomato, and yellow aji pepper, are added and cooked for a few minutes more. Finally, soy sauce, vinegar, and beef broth are added, and the mixture is cooked until the sauce thickens slightly. It is served with french fries and white rice.

step-by-step

Ingridients
  • 1 pound of beef tenderloin, cut into thin strips
  • 2 cloves of garlic, finely chopped
  • Salt to taste
  • Black pepper to taste
  • 3 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 1 small red onion, cut into thick slices
  • 2 plum tomatoes, cut into thick slices
  • 1 yellow aji pepper, seeded and deveined, cut into thin strips
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 3 tablespoons of red wine vinegar
  • 1/3 cup of beef broth
  • 1/2 cup of chopped fresh cilantro
  • 2 cups of french fries
  • White rice as a side dish

Instructions

Step 1
  1. Season the beef with salt, pepper, and chopped garlic.
  2. Heat the oil in a wok or large skillet over high heat.
  3. Sauté the beef in batches until browned on all sides. Set aside.
Step 2
  1. In the same skillet, sauté the onion and tomato for a few minutes.
  2. Add the yellow aji pepper and cook for another minute.
  3. Add the soy sauce, vinegar, and beef broth. Cook until the sauce thickens slightly.
Step 3
  1. Return the beef to the skillet and mix with the sauce.
  2. Add the chopped cilantro and cook for another minute.
  3. Serve immediately with white rice and french fries.
Tips
  • You can use another cut of beef, such as sirloin or filet mignon.
  • If you don't have yellow aji pepper, you can use habanero or serrano pepper.
  • You can add other vegetables to the stir-fry, such as peppers or mushrooms.
  • You can use homemade or frozen french fries.
Lomo saltado is a flavorful and hearty dish, perfect for a family meal.
